\chapter{Pseudocode}
\label{app:pseudocode}

To include pseudocode in your project, use the \texttt{algorithmic} environment (place inside an \texttt{algorithm} environment for figure-like floating):
%
\begin{verbatim}
\begin{algorithm}
  \begin{algorithmic}
    % pseudocode here...
  \end{algorithmic}
  \caption{algorithm title}
  \label{alg:label}
\end{algorithm}
\end{verbatim}
